What is DALS?

DALS a social experiment allowing pet lovers world wide to own and share a fun and playful digital token. Dalmatians is a decentralized peer-to-peer digital token that can be traded and exchanged with other community members.

Where can I store my DALS?

DALS is an erc20 token built on the Ethereum blockchain. DALS can be stored in any Ethereum Wallet.

Where can I trade DALS?

DALS can be traded between Pet loving Dalmatians members world wide as well as participating centralized and decentralized exchanges.
